Our History

Bloom & Bold began in 2016 as a mobile flower truck serving local farmers' markets. Founded by floral designer Treasure Ndinisa, we quickly gained a reputation for our unique approach to floral artistry. By 2021, we proudly opened our first brick-and-mortar retail space, and we are now expanding to offer a seamless digital experience.
